Privacy policy

How SebsWebs handles enquiry information.

Last updated: 28 June 2026. This page explains what information SebsWebs may receive when someone asks for a quote or works on a website project.

Who handles the information

SebsWebs is run by Seb Banham. For privacy questions, email sebybanham@gmail.com.

Information collected

  • Name and contact details sent by email or quote request.
  • Business type, website needs, project notes and messages.
  • Files or content supplied for a website project, such as logos, photos, page text and business details.
  • Basic website/account information needed to launch or support a client site, where the client chooses to share it.

How information is used

Information is used to reply to enquiries, prepare quotes, deliver website projects, manage support requests, keep business records and improve SebsWebs services. SebsWebs does not sell personal information.

Sharing and storage

Information may be stored in email, documents, spreadsheets, project folders or website tools used to deliver the work. Quote form submissions are handled through FormSubmit before being sent to SebsWebs by email. Information may also be shared with third-party services only when needed for hosting, domains, email, forms, analytics or project delivery.

Cookies and analytics

The current SebsWebs site is intended to be simple and does not use analytics cookies. The quote form may use basic spam-protection features from the form provider. If analytics are added later, this privacy page should be updated.

How long information is kept

Enquiry information may be kept while replying and following up. Client project and payment records may be kept for business record-keeping. Information that is no longer needed should be deleted or archived securely.

Your rights

You can ask what information is held about you, ask for corrections, or ask for information to be deleted where legally possible. Email sebybanham@gmail.com to make a request.
